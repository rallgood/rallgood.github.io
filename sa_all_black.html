SimplyAgree was started by a lawyer frustrated by the state of legal software tools.

About Us...

SimplyAgree builds on years of client-attorney interactions and experience. We know the frustrations that both lawyers and clients feel. Ironically, they often overlap.

SimplyAgree was created to improve the process of drafting contracts. SimplyAgree takes dozens to hundreds of contract files and creates a clean, customized template with all of that variation presented unobtrusively. SimplyAgree allows lawyers to spend less time searching and sifting through document files, and more time using their legal expertise to meet client needs. More efficient contracting means happier clients.

What frustrations do you feel in your client-attorney relationship? Let's solve this problem together.

Services...

You've drafted countless contracts, many of the same form. Rather than use your non-billable time, let us capture your most commonly used clauses and save them in an up-to-date, ready-to-use form.

Ever feel like you've done this work before? Don't sweat it; we've captured your previously drafted clauses for quick reference and easy insertion into your standard form.

Use our comparison tool to measure contracts with your standard. Compare different versions of the same contract and, we'll store the drafts to maintain version control during negotiations.
