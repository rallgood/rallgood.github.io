About SimplyAgree

For many attorneys, drafting a new contract can be frustrating and unnecessarily time-consuming. The process often starts by opening up previously used files in Microsoft Word, then searching, comparing, copying, pasting, and reformatting.

SimplyAgree is a secure platform—designed for lawyers—that improves the process of drafting and finalizing contracts. SimplyAgree analyzes dozens to hundreds of contract files, then (1) creates a custom template and (2) captures all of the variation between contracts. All of this information is then automatically organized into a single document. SimplyAgree allows lawyers to spend less time searching and sifting through document files, and more time using their legal expertise to meet client needs.

Services

You've drafted countless contracts, many of the same form. Rather than use your non-billable time, let SimplyAgree capture your most commonly used clauses and save them in an up-to-date, ready-to-use form.

The best contracts are not simply forms, but documents that are created to meet the specific needs of each client. That's why SimplyAgree captures your previously drafted clauses for quick reference and easy insertion into your standard form.

Use our comparison tool to measure contracts with your standard. Compare different versions of the same contract and, we'll store the drafts to maintain version control during negotiations.
